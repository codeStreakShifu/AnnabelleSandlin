Beyond Barriers


This book follows David Davey, whose life changed after contracting polio in 1948. Experience his journey as a 12-year-old in an iron lung, discovering his God-given purpose. See how he became a Christian leader, transforming Goodwill Industries and advocating for disability rights. As a key liaison, Dave played a vital role in assisting Senator Bob Dole’s office in passing the Disabilities Act.
